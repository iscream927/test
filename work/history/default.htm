The Objective Truth Project

Brochure, Web, and Merchandise Design (*Student Project* no association with the History Channel)
Project Overview
The Objective Truth Project explores the cyclical nature of history, how we’re being taught history, and how we should absorb it. The overarching message is about how society today needs to put more brain power into how we consume history and turn a critical eye on historical interpretation and bias. As a student project, I envisioned this campaign in collaboration with the History Channel. Through their platforms, this information can be brought to a wider audience while also driving interest toward their website.
Process Book
Goals and Objectives
The objective of this campaign is to educate and encourage young Americans to rethink how they’ve viewed history (a boring unnecessary subject) and learn to use it as a tool to better understand the world around them.
